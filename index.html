China Watch · China

Washington and Beijing are sparring — and still making plans

Lingling Wei's read on the week: Trump's claim that China compromised 220 million US voter files, a new 90-day visa cap on Chinese journalists, and the detention of an American scholar in Kunming all point to friction — yet neither side wants a rupture.
