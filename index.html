· Australia

AI has changed what criminals can do with your stolen Origin data

Origin Energy is still racing to determine the scale of the breach affecting its 4.8 million customers, and cyber experts warn the stolen names, addresses and phone numbers are more dangerous than they look — AI can now clone a voice for WhatsApp calls, pass identity checks and authorise fraudulent transactions. UNSW's Professor Richard Buckler says the biggest risk is opportunistic scammers exploiting anxiety about the breach itself: be "alert but not alarmed" and avoid unsolicited links.

US security concerns over Chinese research ships that visit Australia
